Hello World!

My name is Tiffini Tobiasson.

I am currently a student enrolled at the Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago, Illinois. I am studying Computer Science and minoring in Internet Application Development. My hopes after graduating in Spring of 2019 are to attend the Institute of Design to persue a Masters in Design.

I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona where I went to school at Vail Academy and High School in the Vail School District. In high school I was heavily involved in my FIRST Robotics team; Team 1828 The BoxerBots. My involvement in FIRST in high school is what inspired me to come to Illinois Tech and persue a career in a STEM field. I am still heavily involved in FIRST today as a mentor to team 5125, Hawks on the Horizon as well as a volunteer for FRC and FTC events.

As far as my on campus involvement goes outside of taking classes, I currently have an on campus job with the IPRO Department. I am also involved in one of the sororities on my campus, Alpha Sigma Alpha (ASA). I rushed ASA in Spring of 2015 and have been an active member ever since. This semester I picked up the position of Social Media and Webmaster. So in total, I am now managing three different websites!

  • and this one you are currently on!

Outside of school, work and on campus extra cirricular activities, I like to spend my free time doing a lot of odd ball things. I enjoy hula hooping as a form of exercise, and yes, it can be a lot more complicated than simply moving the hoop around your hips. I also have many crafting hobbies including: cross stitching, crocheting, knitting, painting and wire wraping.

My biggest passion though is listening to EDM Music and attending music festivals and raves. I began raving and going to festivals in the Summer of 2015 and fell in love with the culture and the amazing people that I have been able to meet. Since beginning my festival journey I have attended Spring Awakening, Mamby, Summer Set, Freaky Deaky, EDC Las Vegas, North Coast and Basscenter. This summer I will be attending Spring Awakening (for the third year in a row), Electric Forest, and Basscenter.
